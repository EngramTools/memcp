## Decisions Made

- **Per-sample checkpoint granularity:** Checkpointing after each full sample (not each QA pair) keeps the checkpoint file small and avoids partial-sample state. A failed sample can be retried from scratch — ingest is cheap compared to LLM calls.
- **Abstention phrase list:** 12 phrases checked case-insensitively. Sufficient for the adversarial category where the model is expected to say "I don't know" explicitly.
- **LoCoMo report metrics:** Both `overall_f1` (flat mean of all questions) and `task_averaged_f1` (mean of per-category means) reported. History entry uses `overall_f1` as `overall_score` and `task_averaged_f1` as `task_averaged_score` — consistent with LongMemEval's accuracy fields.
